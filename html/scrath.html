Zion National Park

Hikes in Zion

  • Angles Landing
  • Emerald Falls
  • The Narrows

Instructions on how to get inside the park:

  1. Drive to the parking lot
    • If it is the summer time, take a shuttle
    • If it is the winter time, you can use your car
  2. Use park pass or pay the fee to enter the park
  3. Find a hike you enjoy and have fun!
